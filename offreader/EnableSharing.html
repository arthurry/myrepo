Enable Sharing

Follow these simple steps to enable OffReader iOS Extension.

  1. Tap the Share icon on the bottom of your screen.
  2. Swipe the colored app icons, and tap More.
  3. Tap Edit, then the plus sign next to OffReader, and finally tap Done.
  4. Now, you can tap the Pocket icon to save, from Safari or any app using the Share button.

Now you can save from Safari, or anywhere you see the share menu!
